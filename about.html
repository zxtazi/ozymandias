About, Me:

This site serves to function as a freeform journal of sorts. a place to dump my thoughts under the illusion of anonymity, to back up my delusional claims of grandeur, and to serve as a portfoloio. As I learn to build this site better, I'll be adding a seperate page for photos, for art, and for a reading list. Hopefully, my disjointed thoughts serve some sort of purpose; if nothing else, I'll be entertained.
